Restaurateur App

Developed in Spring 2014 as a project for CSE 110 over the span of 10 weeks, a group of 10 developers formed team D.O.G.E. (Designs Oriented for Gourmet Establishments) to design and create an Android app to help owners and managers. Functions focus on management of employees, the work schedule, and inventory.
